Overview

  • "Pushpa: The Rule" is the sequel to the blockbuster film "Pushpa: The Rise".
  • Directed by Sukumar, it continues the story of Pushpa Raj, a red sandalwood smuggler.
  • The film features gripping drama, intense action sequences, and character development.
